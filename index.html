Working Open Workshop

https://mzl.la/2017wow

At our second Working Open Workshop, Mozilla Science Lab staff, Fellows and community will run a set of trainings to help prepare open science projects for a successful Global Sprint in June 2017. This page will serve as a resource for all session materials.

Schedule

The schedule can be found in the workshop repository here, the following handouts are ordered according to the scheduled session times.

Presentations + Exercises

Each session has a ten-minute presentation. Slides for these presentations are linked below. Exercises can be completed during work sessions.

May 8, 2017

  • 9:00-9:15 - Welcome + Introductions - code of conduct and plan for the day
  • 9:15-10:00 - What is open? - Define openness, explore the Network. - Exercise
  • 10:15-10:30 - Open Canvas - create a coherent strategy, including channels for contribution. - Exercise
  • 10:30-10:45 - README - describe and dejargonize your project. - Exercise
  • 10:45-11:00 - Roadmapping - plan your project schedule and priorities. - Exercise
  • 11:00-11:30 - Contributor Guidelines + Code of Conduct - facilitate contribution. - Ex.-Guidelines Ex.-CoC
  • 11:30-12:00 - Personas and Pathways - plan for your project users and their participation. - Exercise
  • 12:00-1:00 - BOF - TOPIC - talk about some topic.
  • 12:00-1:00 - BOF - TOPIC 2 - talk about some topic.
  • 1:00-1:30 - Community Building Roundtable - develop plans for a variety of communities.
  • 1:30-4:00 - What should we do in Austin - Plan - develop plans for this local of community.
  • 4:15-5:30 - Running your own WOW - develop plans for hosting a future WOW.

Glossary

OLC

Open Leadership Cohort is a group of participants who have a shared experience and goal - to help further open practice in their work and community. It's what the WOW participants will be inducted into, you can read more about it on this blog.
